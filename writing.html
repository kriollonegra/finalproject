Sakriléjiu: My Stolen Pineapple

An environment with many eyes on you.

Certain expectations of being, unrealistic in expectation and inevitable bad intentions.

Ultimately you did not fit the mold - as a child - and continue to never fit into this.

What arose was stifling and odd. And what is born from that is the transformation from folding up to biting back.

(August 3rd, 2021)
